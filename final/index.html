About Me

Hello! I'm Lea Li, a passionate and dedicated web design enthusiast with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SASS. My journey began during my coursework at San Diego Mesa College, where I discovered my love for crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites.

Over the years, I have developed a keen eye for design, striving to create modern, responsive layouts that adapt seamlessly to all devices. From working on small projects like interactive galleries to more advanced assignments integrating Bootstrap and SASS, I have consistently improved my skills and learned new techniques.
